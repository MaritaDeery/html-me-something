Shopping Guide

Rome is one of the best cities in the world to shop. The availabilty of gorgeous clothing and accessories is rivaled only by the offerings of New York City. One of my favorite things to do is to sit in a piazza and admire the polish and flair of the Italians as they stroll through the city.

Sites

One of my favorite places to visit is Italy. I have been several times, covered most of the country, and concur with the old adage that "All roads lead to Rome." Rome is one of the most fabulous cities in the world!

There are many lovely sites to see in Rome. Aside from ancient edifices, there are many other wonderful things to see. The Borghese Gardens is one of my favorite places.





Restaurant Guide

Italy also offers wonderful cuisine. There is nothing more enjoyable than partaking of the local faire after a long day of shopping!
